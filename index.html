How It Works

NovaSTEM delivers interactive, age-appropriate experiments across physics, chemistry, and biology—designed to make abstract concepts tangible through hands-on exploration.

Our Impact

NovaSTEM workshops foster curiosity, teamwork, and early scientific confidence through engaging, hands-on experiences.

About the Founder

NovaSTEM was founded to make high-quality STEM education accessible, engaging, and inspiring for young students. By turning curiosity into hands-on exploration, NovaSTEM helps students see science as something they can actively understand and enjoy.
